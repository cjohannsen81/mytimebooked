Great work is invisible.
That's the whole problem.

You come home and can't tell if the pool chemistry is right or what got cleaned. Your pro did their best work for an empty house — nobody saw it, nobody said anything. And telling your housekeeper she missed a spot is so awkward that most people just quietly quit. So every job now ends with a card.

Your reply — two taps, zero awkwardness

Warmth is easy to send. And "one small thing" arrives as a note on the next visit's briefing — not a confrontation, not a public review.

For the proEvery card joins a public portfolio of real work — craft you can point to, not a star average. Tips land while the pride is fresh. And "one small thing" reads as coaching, not a strike.
For the customerYou finally see what you paid for — including what you'd never notice, like "your filter is on its last season." The card is proof, receipt, and relationship in one.
