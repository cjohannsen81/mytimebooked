EXPERIMENT — 10

The Walkthrough

Film the job · quotes in a human voice

"…and it's worse behind the shed."

Nobody can describe a lawn, and pros drive across town to quote jobs they don't get. Film 47 seconds instead — quotes come back as voice notes, priced off your actual mess.

SHOW, DON'T TELL → EXPERIMENT — 11

The Block

Neighbors pool · everyone pays less

"if it's the same morning, we're in"

Six houses, six drives, six minimum fees — one street. When the block books the same pro the same morning, prices drop for everyone and the pro still earns more. Watch the street join in.

KNOCK ON THE BLOCK → EXPERIMENT — 12

Send Help

Care as a gift · dignity built in

"Send help, not flowers."

Your mom won't ask. The new parents say they're fine. Send housekeeping, lawn care, or a sitter as a gift the recipient controls — they approve it, set the times, and can swap it. Siblings split the cost in one tap.

The House Plan
